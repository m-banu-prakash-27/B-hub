Frequently Asked Questions

What is the admission process for B-Hub?

The admission process at B-Hub involves completing an online application, taking our entrance exam that assesses your aptitude and business acumen, followed by a personal interview with our faculty panel. Final selections are made based on your overall performance across these stages.

How is B-Hub different from regular colleges?

Unlike traditional colleges, B-Hub offers an integrated program that combines BBA academics with CAT preparation and leadership development. We focus on practical skills, industry exposure, and building a global mindset. Our selective cohort of just 40 students ensures personalized attention and a high-performance environment.

Does B-Hub provide placement assistance?

Yes, B-Hub provides comprehensive placement support. Our program is designed to prepare students for multiple career pathways: securing admission to top B-schools through CAT, obtaining placement in leading companies, or pursuing entrepreneurial ventures. We have strong industry connections and provide internship opportunities, career counseling, and placement assistance.

What are the fees and scholarship options at B-Hub?

B-Hub offers competitive fee structures with various payment options. We also provide merit-based scholarships to exceptional students. For detailed information about fees and scholarship opportunities, please contact our admissions office.

Can I join B-Hub after completing 12th grade?

Yes, B-Hub is specifically designed as a post-12th education model. Students who have completed their 12th grade with any stream (Science, Commerce, or Arts) are eligible to apply. We look for ambitious students with strong academic backgrounds and leadership potential.
