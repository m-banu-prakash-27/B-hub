CAT Preparation Program

Our specialized CAT preparation program is meticulously designed to help students excel in the Common Admission Test and secure admission to top B-schools in India.

Comprehensive Coverage:

  • Quantitative Ability (QA): Number Systems, Algebra, Arithmetic, Geometry, Modern Math
  • Data Interpretation & Logical Reasoning (DILR): Tables, Graphs, Charts, Puzzles, Arrangements, Logic
  • Verbal Ability & Reading Comprehension (VARC): Reading Comprehension, Grammar, Vocabulary, Verbal Logic

Test Preparation:

  • Regular sectional tests
  • Full-length mock CATs
  • Personalized performance analysis
  • Improvement strategies
  • Time management techniques

Post-CAT Preparation:

  • Group Discussion preparation
  • Personal Interview training
  • Written Ability Test (WAT) practice
  • B-school selection guidance

Program Timeline

Semester 1

Foundation courses in business, introduction to CAT preparation, and basic leadership development workshops.

Semester 2

Core business subjects, focused CAT quantitative training, and communication skills development.

Semester 3

Advanced business courses, verbal ability and reading comprehension for CAT, and business thinking workshops.

Semester 4

Specialized business subjects, data interpretation and logical reasoning for CAT, and professional development training.

Semester 5

Industry internship, intensive CAT mock tests, and interview preparation workshops.

Semester 6

Final business projects, CAT exam, and B-school selection and preparation.
